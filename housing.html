Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Board of Housing Authority


What is the Board of Housing Authority?

The Board of Housing Authority manages programs that provide housing asssitance to low-income families, veterans, and the elderly.


What does the Board of Housing Authority do?

The Board of Housing Authority, managing a total of 256 units of conventional housing for veterans, families, eldery, handicapped people, and the developmentally disabled. They also administer numerous vouchers and certificates.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, one candidate.
