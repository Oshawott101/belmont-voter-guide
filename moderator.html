Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Moderator


What is a Moderator?

The moderator oversees over town meetings and sometimes other municpal meeting.


What does a Moderator do?

The moderator regulates proceedings in a town meeting, decides the order of questions covered, and makes public declarations of all vote results, and may administer the oath of office to any town officer chosen at the meeting.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, one candidate.


Who is running for Moderator?
