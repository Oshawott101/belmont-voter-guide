Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Board of Cemetery Commissioners


What is the Board of Cemetery Commissioners?

The Board of Cemetery Commissioners, under the supervision of the Board of Selectmen, maintains public cemeteries.


What does the Board of Cemetery Commissioners do?

The Board of Cemetery Commissioners maintain the cemeteries and appoint employees as necessary to maintain the cemeteries around Belmont.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, one candidate.
