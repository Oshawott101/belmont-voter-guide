Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Board of Health


What is the Board of Health?

The Board of Health is the policy-making, rule-making, and adjudicatory body for public health.


What does the Board of Health do?

The Board of Health creates rules, and policies regarding public health, and influence day-to-day local public health agency administration.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, one candidate.
