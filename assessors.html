Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Board of Assessors


What is the Board of Assessors?

The Board of Assessors reevaluate all properties of residence or commerce.


What does the Board of Assessors do?

The Board of Assessors lists and values all private and public property, and reviews property sales each year to determine if the assessed values should be changed.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, one candidate.
