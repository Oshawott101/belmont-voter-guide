What is the Select Board?

The Select Board, also called the Board of Selectman, acts as the executive arm of the town government.


What does the Select Board do?

The Select Board calls town meetings, proposes budgets in the town meetings, creates basic regulations, sets public policies, and nominates positions on numerous other committees, including the, among other functions.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, one candidate.
