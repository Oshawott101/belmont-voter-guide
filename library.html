Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Trustees of the Public Library


What are the Trustees of the Public Library?

The Trustees of the Public Library govern the Belmont Public Library.


What does the Trustees of the Public Library do?

The Trustees of the Public Library oversee the library operations, budget, as well as numerous programs, including Friends of the Belmont Public Library.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, two candidates.


Who is Running for Trustees of the Public Library?

  • Elaine C. Alligood (Incumbent)
  • Corinne McCue Olmsted (Incumbent)
