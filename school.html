Guide To The April 6th Ballot: Members of the School Committee


What is the School Committee?

The School Committee is the policy setting entity for the public schools in our district.


What does the School Committee do?

The School Committee appoints the Superintendent, approves several other key positions, creates educational goals for all schools in our district, and negotiates with school union. While not able to create a budget, they decide how to spend the money they do recieve.


How many candidates can you vote for?

You can vote for, at most, two candidates.
